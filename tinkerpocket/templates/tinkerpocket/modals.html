About TinkePocket

What the heck is this?

Tinkerpocket is a player-made tool for the science-fiction MMORPG Anarchy Online. In February of 2023, one of AO's greatest resources, http://aopocket.tngk.com went offline. While we all hope this is a temporary situation, Tinkerpocket serves as a less-awesome backup to make that information available to players until it returns.

How accurate is it?

As accurate as I can make it using the best information the AO player community has to offer. I won't claim that it's perfect in every case, though, so if you find an inaccuracy, please get in touch and I'll get it fixed.

I need to contact you!

Great! I can be found on the official Anarchy Online discord as TinkeringIdiot#7251 (@TinkeringIdiot), or you can email me at admin(AT)tinkeringidiot.com.

What's your privacy policy?

Tinkerpocket doesn't gather or store any information about you. There's no login or account, and there never will be. There is a session that stores only the profile you specify in the tool, and that is wiped after 6 hours of inactivity. Google Analytics is used to monitor visitor statistics, their privacy policy is here.
