About Tinkernanos

What the heck is this?

Tinkernanos is a player-made tool for the science-fiction MMORPG Anarchy Online. In 2023, the popular AO database site AOItems went offline for an extended period, and took the best resource for listing nano programs with it. I've set out to create an updated, accurate tool that allows players to easily find the nanos they need.

How accurate is it?

As accurate as I can make it all by my little lonesome. I won't claim that it's perfect in every case, though, so if you find an inaccuracy, please get in touch and I'll get it fixed.

I need to contact you!

Great! I can be found on the official Anarchy Online discord as TinkeringIdiot#7251 (@TinkeringIdiot), or you can email me at admin(AT)tinkeringidiot.com.

What's your privacy policy?

Tinkernukes doesn't gather or store any information about you. There's no login or account, and there never will be. There is a session that stores only the profile you specify in the tool, and that is wiped after 6 hours of inactivity. Google Analytics is used to monitor visitor statistics, their privacy policy is here.
