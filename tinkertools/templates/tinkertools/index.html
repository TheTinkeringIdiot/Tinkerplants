Welcome to TinkerTools!
 
TinkerTools are a set of utilities and helpers for the Sci-Fi MMORPG Anarchy Online. They are provided free of charge to the player community in the hope that they can enhance the joy of playing one of the most complex and fun video games on Earth.
 
Current Time on Rubi-Ka (Athen Standard Time)
 
Tinkerplants In 2022, many existing implant design tools are either majorly out-of-date, no longer supported, or require download and installation. I've set out to create an updated, accurate tool that allows players to plan out their implants in a web-browser, with a few extra features that I've always wished other tools supported (JOBE clusters, refined implants, QL bumping, etc).
 
Tinkernukes After many years, existing Nanotechnician nuke tables are either majorly out-of-date, no longer supported, or have disappeared entirely. I've attempted to create an updated, accurate tool that allows NT players to quickly and easily identify nukes that will perform well for their current build and abilities.
 
Tinkerfite One of the outstanding difficulties in Anarchy Online is deciding which weapon to use. With over 10,000 weapons available, the "right" choice isn't always clear...assuming you even know what all the options are. Tinkerfite sets out to help alleviate that problem by suggesting weapons you can use based on your stats and skills, so you're always making an informed choice.
 
Tinkerpocket In February of 2023, one of AO's greatest resources, http://aopocket.tngk.com went offline. While we all hope this is a temporary situation, Tinkerpocket serves as a less-awesome backup to make that information available to players until it returns.
