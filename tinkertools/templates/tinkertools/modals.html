About TinkerItems

What the heck is this?

TinkerItems is a player-made tool for the science-fiction MMORPG Anarchy Online. With over 100,000 items and nanoprograms available in the game, it can be difficult to find the right one for your character. TinkerItems aims to make that easier by providing a simple, fast, and accurate search tool for the game's items.

Where does the data come from?

Straight from the game client's very own database. I've created a tool, TinkerParse (based on Demoder's excellent AODb repo), that parses and dumps the game's database, which is then made available for you to look up here.

What's your privacy policy?

TinkerItems doesn't gather or store any information about you. There's no login or account, and there never will be. There is a session that stores only the profile you specify in the tool, and that is wiped after 6 hours of inactivity. Google Analytics is used to monitor visitor statistics, their privacy policy is here.
