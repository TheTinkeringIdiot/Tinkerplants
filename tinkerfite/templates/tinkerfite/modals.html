About TinkerFite

What the heck is this?

Tinkerfite is a player-made tool for the science-fiction MMORPG Anarchy Online. One of the outstanding difficulties in Anarchy Online is deciding which weapon to use. With over 10,000 weapons available, the "right" choice isn't always clear...assuming you eve know what all the options are. Tinkerfite sets out to help alleviate that problem by suggesting weapons you can use based on your stats and skills, so you're always making an informed choice.

How accurate is it?

As accurate as I can make it using the best information the AO player community has to offer. I won't claim that it's perfect in every case, though, so if you find an inaccuracy, please get in touch and I'll get it fixed. The DPS calculations make some necessary assumptions (all attacks land and never miss, for example), and some educated guesses (attack rating bonus > 1000) and so are a little idealized, but still reasonable.

What's included in the DPS?

The DPS calculation accounts for weapon hits, critical strikes, attack rating bonus, added damage, and special attacks. Damage output is calculated for a 60 second window, and divided to give damage per seecond.

What's your privacy policy?

Tinkerfite doesn't gather or store any information about you. There's no login or account, and there never will be. There is a session that stores only the profile you specify in the tool, and that is wiped after 6 hours of inactivity. Google Analytics is used to monitor visitor statistics, their privacy policy is here.
